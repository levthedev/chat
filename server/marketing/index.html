Features
Open Source
HumbleChat is 100% open source. You are welcome to host it on your own server for free. Plus, you can modify HumbleChat to suit your own needs.
Powerful Chat
HumbleChat comes fully featured with typing, delivery status, and online status indicators. You can filter chats, tag them, and even respond on the go with email, Slack, and SMS notifications.
Expressive Analytics
You can view the number of new messages, new users, and closed chats, in addition to your engagement rate and average response time.
User Segments
Create segments based on user behavior, such as pages visited, number of sessions, or time on page. Then, you can automatically send messages or tag users who fit a certain segment.
